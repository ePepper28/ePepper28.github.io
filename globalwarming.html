What is Global Warming?

Global warming is long-term shifts in temperatures and heat caused by natural occurences, fossil fuel emissions, and greenhouse gas emissions.

What is global warming being caused by?

  • Greenhouse Gas Emissions
  • Power generation
  • Manufracturing goods
  • Cutting down rainforests.

What is global warming doing?

  • Changing water availability.
  • Antarctica loses 50 billion tons of ice mass per year.
  • Sea levels rising rapidly.
